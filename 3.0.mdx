# Roll-out
**UpMeet 3.0 is being rolled out in stages** to make the Meet View the best version it can be.
- **Stage 1:** Internal Testing (Current)
- **Stage 2:** Quality Assurance Testing (Starting)
- **Stage 3:** Closed Beta _(Soon)_
- **Stage 4:** General Release _(Soon)_

# Reverting to 2.0
Once enrolled in the 3.0 beta you will be in a seperate environment like app-qa.upmeet.me/something
to switch you can go to your old link.

If it was the same link you may not revert manually and should contact us instead,
