## I am the owner of the meeting
On the end of you're link you should see a `?id=YOUR_ROOM_ID`. 

If you don't you need to add it. If you don't know the room id, please contact reece@upmeet.me for help.

## I am a participant
Unfortunately there is not much you can do but reach out to the owner of the meeting.

If you do contact them please send them to this page so they can solve it faster.
