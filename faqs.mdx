<AccordionGroup>
    <Accordion title="Do you charge per user?">
       No. We charge a monthly flat fee. If you need multiple users or admins contact us at team@upmeet.me.
    </Accordion>

    <Accordion title="Do you have a free plan?">
        No. We currently have a 7-day free trial when you signup to any plan.
    </Accordion>

    <Accordion title="Are my calls secure?">
        100% yes. We believe privacy is a human right therefore it is the first thing we think about before adding any features. We use end to end encryption and use daily to secure your meetings.
    </Accordion>
    
    <Accordion title="Do I need to download anything?">
        No. UpMeet is purely web-based so we can push updates instantly and to provide users with a better experience.
    </Accordion>

    <Accordion title="Which plan should I choose?">
        Depending on your needs you can view our comparison table or email the team at team@upmeet.me and we'll give you our honest opinion.
    </Accordion>
