## What is security at UpMeet?
Data security and privacy is a fundamental human right and we use secure methods to protect your data.

## Are meetings secure?
Yes. Meetings with less than 4 people are end-to-end encrypted otherwise a secure server is used by your meeting room. No-one at UpMeet or otherwise can access your meetings.
