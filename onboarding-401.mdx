## What is Error 401?
Error 401 is when the page does not have appropriate credentials to access the resource. In this case it is the Room ID. 
### If you just signed up
If you just signed up and filled in your billing info, it is most likely on our end. Please email team@upmeet.me and we will fix this for you immediately.
### If you have not just signed up
If you have not just signed up it is best to contact our team at team@upmeet.me to see how we can help. If you're looking to add a integration than look at our docs.
